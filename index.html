How to win:

This is pretty quick on my computer, but it might be very slow on yours. Too bad.

Open another browser window with the real game.

In this window, use the following grid on your keyboard to increment the tile values so that they look like your real game:

1 2 3 4
Q W E R
A S D F
Z X C V

Then press B to calculate your best move.

You can use your arrow keys as usual to move tiles up, down, left, and right, but the game will not place a random tile for you automatically. You can instead use the same keyboard grid to add the new 2 or 4 tile that your real game added, and press B again to calculate the next move.

It would take forever to do this for a whole game. Instead, you should play your real game until you are close to losing (only a couple empty tiles), use this coach until you have no risk of losing (lots of empty tiles), refresh this page, rinse, and repeat.

This is much easier to do if you can fit both windows on the screen at the same time. Put the real game on the right to make your keyboard align better.

How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
